# Funding-Finder
An interactive tool for finding fisheries, harvesting, and economic development funding programs available in Nunavut.

**86 programs** across federal, territorial, regional, and community sources.

## How to Use

Select any combination of filters from the sentence builder:
- **Beneficiary status** — Nunavut Agreement Beneficiary (Inuit) or not
- **Who you are** — GN department, Inuit organization, harvester, business, non-profit, or community
- **Region** — All Nunavut, Qikiqtaaluk, Kivalliq, or Kitikmeot
- **What you need** — equipment, market development, research, training, harvesting support, business start-up, conservation, or processing

Results update instantly. Click any program card to expand full details including eligibility, amounts, deadlines, and contact information.
